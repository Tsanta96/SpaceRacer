Info Page

You have just completed the drop off of the last known bottle of Jack Daniels to the Emporer of Mercury. The solar system is dying and most civilizations have already migrated to a neighboring star community rigged with worm holes, strobe lights, and other strange phenomena. The Sun has been on it's last breath for thousands of years now and at this moment it is shaping up for a massive explosion capable of disintegrating what remains of the solar system. It is now up to you, Captain Stardust, to bring your ship and its occupants to safety by evacuating the solar system before it is too late.......Good Luck!
